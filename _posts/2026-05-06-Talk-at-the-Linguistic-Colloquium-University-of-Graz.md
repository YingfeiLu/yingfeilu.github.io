On May 6, 2026, I gave a talk at the Linguistic Colloquium of the University of Graz with Dr. Zi Huang and Prof. Dr. Fabio Montermini.

We presented *Descriptive and Evaluative Meaning in Spanish Diminutive Prefixes: A Case Study of micro- and mini-*.

Our study examines the semantics of the Spanish prefixes *micro-* and *mini-* using data from the Observatori de Neologia. We propose an evaluativeness scale in which *micro-* is descriptive and evaluatively neutral, *-ito/a* suffixes have a strong evaluative value, and *mini-* occupies an intermediate position that can be descriptive alone or both descriptive and evaluative.

The talk took place at Merangasse 70, SR 33.3.211, Graz.
