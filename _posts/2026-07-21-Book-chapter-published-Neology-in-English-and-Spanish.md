I am delighted to share that my book chapter, *Neology in English and Spanish. 2005–2015. Lexicological Characteristics and Lexicographic Representation*, has now been published.

The chapter is included in *Applied Lexical Studies: From Lexicology to Language Teaching*, a volume that brings together contributions presented at the International Congress on Theoretical and Applied Lexical Studies (CIETAL 2025).

I am pleased to contribute to this volume and to share my research on neology in English and Spanish, focusing on its lexicological characteristics and lexicographic representation.
